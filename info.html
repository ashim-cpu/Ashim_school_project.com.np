The Evolution of Web Technology

Web technology has come a long way since its inception. The World Wide Web, invented by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989, started as a way for researchers to share documents.

By the mid-1990s, the introduction of web browsers like Netscape Navigator allowed people to explore websites graphically. This led to the rapid growth of the internet, turning it into a household necessity.

Modern web technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript frameworks like React and Angular, have revolutionized the way websites and applications are built, offering dynamic, responsive, and interactive experiences.
